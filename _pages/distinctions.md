## Gilles Kahn 2020: National award for best Ph.D. theses in computer science, France.

My Ph.D. thesis was awarded an accessit of the [Gilles Kahn](https://www.ins2i.cnrs.fr/en/node/2442) prize from Société Informatique de France. Established in 1998, this French national award honors outstanding Ph.D. theses in the field of computer science.

## CORE Junior Funding 2021 by FNR, Luxembourg.
My research project, _SpotFlakes: Spotting The Root Causes Of Flaky Tests_, was granted 743,000€ by the Luxembourg National Research Fund (FNR).
